# üìú Legacy's End - Quest Design Bible

> "To fix the world, you must first fix the code."

This document serves as the **Master Plan** for all Quests in Legacy's End. It connects the narrative (Lore) with the gameplay mechanics and provides concrete examples for creating future chapters.

---

## üß≠ Design Philosophy

Every Quest follows a strict **"Refactoring Ark"**:
1.  **The Broken World (Legacy)**: The player encounters a world that works but is fragile, chaotic, or cursed.
2.  **The Revelation (Concept)**: Alarion (the player) learns a new ancient truth (Encapsulation, Sovereignty, Flow).
3.  **The Transformation (Refactor)**: The player applies this truth, healing the world and earning a badge.

### 1. üõ°Ô∏è The Aura of Sovereignty
**Focus:** Boundaries & Protection.
**Status:** ‚úÖ Playable

*   **Scenario (Flavor):** **The Toxic Swamp**. A bubbling, chaotic mire where everything is stained by "Global Pollution" (pink/red slime).
*   **NPC:** **The Shield-Smith**. A hermit living in a hermetically sealed bubble. *Dialogue*: "Without boundaries, we are but soup."
*   **The Problem:** The hero's armor changes color randomly because the swamp's curse "bleeds" into everything.
*   **The Solution:** Alarion mustforge a "Shadow Shield" that isolates his gear from the swamp's influence.
*   **Reward:** **Badge of the Bubble**. Ability: "Style Immunity" - The hero no longer turns pink when walking through global slime.

### 2. üîÆ The Orb of Inquiry
**Focus:** Independence & Supply.
**Status:** ‚úÖ Playable

*   **Scenario (Flavor):** **The Chain Gang Prison**. Characters are physically chained to heavy anvils labeled "Database" or "Network".
*   **NPC:** **The Liberator**. A rogue who picks locks. *Dialogue*: "Why carry the world when you can ask the Universe to provide it?"
*   **The Problem:** Villagers are crushed by the weight of their tools (Dependencies), unable to move or change jobs.
*   **The Solution:** Alarion learns the "Ritual of Context", allowing tools to be summoned magically when needed, breaking the physical chains.
*   **Reward:** **The Ghost Key**. Ability: "Summon Service" - Can invoke tools without carrying them.

### 3. üíé The Flowing Heartstone
**Focus:** Energy Flow & Reaction.
**Status:** üöß Coming Soon

*   **Scenario (Flavor):** **The Stuttering Kingdom**. Time freezes randomly (lag) and people repeat sentences (inefficiency).
*   **NPC:** **The Pulse Watcher**. A monk meditating on a single floating rock. *Dialogue*: "Do not shout to the mountain. Whisper to the stone, and the mountain will move."
*   **The Problem:** The King shouts orders to every citizen individually (Manual Updates), causing chaos and delay.
*   **The Solution:** Alarion constructs a "Signal Network" where citizens react instantly to changes in the Kingdom's state without being told.
*   **Reward:** **The Synced Heart**. Ability: "Flow State" - Movement becomes perfectly smooth.

### 4. üé® The Chromatic Loom
**Focus:** Perception & Themes.
**Status:** üöß Coming Soon

*   **Scenario (Flavor):** **The Monochrome Grey-Lands**. A world drained of color, flat and lifeless.
*   **NPC:** **The Weaver of Light**. A spider-like entity spinning threads of pure RGB. *Dialogue*: "Color is not paint. It is a value passed by reference."

*   **The Solution:** Alarion weaves "Tokens" of variable light, allowing the world to shift from Day to Night instantly.
*   **Reward:** **The Prism Cloak**. Ability: "Theme Shift" - Can toggle day/night cycles at will.

### 6. üëÅÔ∏è The Crimson Altar

**Status:** üöß Coming Soon

*   **Scenario (Flavor):** **The Void Edges**. Places where the world geometry simply ends into white nothingness.

## üõ†Ô∏è Creating New Quests

1.  **Define the Lesson**: What concept is Alarion learning?
2.  **Create the "Broken" State**: How does the world suffer without this concept?
3.  **Define the "Healed" State**: How does the world look when fixed?
4.  **Write the Narrative**:
    *   Alarion enters a room...
    *   The "Monster" is the bad practice.
    *   The "Spell" is the best practice.
5.  **Design the Badge**: What symbol represents this mastery?
